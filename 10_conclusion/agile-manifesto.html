New Manifesto for Agile Software Development




We are uncovering better ways of developing
software by doing it and helping others do it.
Through this work we have come to value:

Individuals and interactions over ceremonies and post-its
 Working software over green bar
 Customer collaboration over stories validation
 Responding to change over successfull iterations

That is, while there is value in the items on
the right, we value the items on the left more.
